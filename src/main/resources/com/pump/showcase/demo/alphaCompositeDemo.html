This demo shows how different AlphaComposites render.

It's one thing to read the AlphaComposite javadoc to understand mathematically what each composite does, but sometimes its easier just to render it to help visualize the difference between SRC_ATOP and DST_OVER.

This demo does not feature any custom code from the Pumpernickel codebase. It's just here to help visualize what the AlphaComposite does on its own.
