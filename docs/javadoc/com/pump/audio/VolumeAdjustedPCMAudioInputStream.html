      • getVolume

        protected abstract float getVolume(float time)
        Return the volume multiplier for a given time in this AudioInputStream.
        Parameters:
        time - the time (in seconds) to retrieve the volume for
        Returns:
        the volume multiplier. For example: 0=silent, .5=half volume, 1.0=normal volume, 2.0=double volume.

        The method filterFrames(..) calls this method and imposes a minimum and maximum on the returned value, so if you double or quintuple the volume: you may get distorted audio but you will not be encoding invalid PCM values.
